About Natalie

My journey began with special effects makeup and flourished during my years as MAC Cosmetics' event specialist at Harrods. But what I've learned goes far beyond technique – it's about understanding the person beneath the makeup, their dreams, their anxieties, and their unique spark. Every face I work with tells a story, and my role is to help that story shine through.

While my kit contains premium products for all skin types, colors, and styles, my most important tool is listening. I believe true beauty emerges when someone feels completely comfortable in their own skin. My approach is about building confidence from within, creating a space where you can explore and celebrate your authentic self. Whether you're preparing for your wedding day or seeking a fresh perspective, we'll work together to discover the look that makes you feel unmistakably, beautifully you.
